Roole Community

There are a few places for you to hang out with fellow Roole users.

Stack Overflow

Ask any general questions on Stack Overflow. You don't need to register an account to ask. Remember to tag the question with roole.

Issue Tracker

Feel free to open an issue on the issue tracker, if you find any bugs, or Roole doesn't work as you intended.

IRC

If you need quick help and advice, join the #roole channel on irc.freenode.net to talk to fellow Roole users in real-time.

Chat directly in the browser, if you don't have an IRC client installed.

Twitter

You should follow Glen Huang, the creator of Roole, for news about Roole.
